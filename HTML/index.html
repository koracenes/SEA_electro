Who We Are

SEA d.o.o. was founded in 2001. The activity of our company is the construction and reconstruction of:

  • Power line voltage level: 10kV, 20kV, 35kV ...
  • Substations 35/10kV, 20/0.4kV, 10/0.4kV
  • Low-voltage networks 0.4 kV
  • Construction and reconstruction of public lighting

Our Power Grid Services

We specialize in electrical infrastructure, including transmission lines, substations, and low-voltage networks.

Design and installation of 110kV and 220kV transmission lines.

Substations

Building and maintaining transformer substations for efficient power distribution.

Low-Voltage Networks
